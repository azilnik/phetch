Phetch in 3 easy steps

Step 1: Send a Phetch

  • Snap a photo!
  • Erase a part of of it (Say a face, a butt, whatever)
  • Choose a friend to send it to!

Step 2: Get a Phetch

  • Receive a photo from a friend with a part missing
  • Fill it in with a photo of your own (A selfie, your dog, something shiny)
  • Share it with the world

Step 3: Hilarity ensues

  • Scroll through your friends’ photos.
  • Some will be awesome, some will be weird.
  • Like the ones that tickle you.
