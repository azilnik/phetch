Snap. Etch. Replace. Share.

Phetch in four easy steps
»

1

Snap

It starts out innocently enough:
Get inspired and snap a photo to send to a friend.

2

Etch

Erase a part of of it – a face, a butt, an ice cream cone – whatever strikes your fancy. Send it to a friend!

3

Replace

Your friend receives your Phetch.
They fill it in with a photo of their own. What will it be? A selfie? Their dog? Something shiny?

4

Share

They share the Phetch and voilà: instant fun! Some of your friends' posts will be hilarious. One might be the next Ellen selfie or win a Nobel Peace Prize. Some might be... NSFW. Oh well.
Be sure to like your favorites.
